# CombatProgressionRework

This mod should only be used on new save files.

## Progression Overview

1. Reach the bottom of the mines. There you receive the key to willy's backroom.
2. Build the boat with reduced material cost.
3. Reach the top of the volcano. There you receive the skull key.
   1. Acquire dwarven or dragontooth weaponry.
4. Reach level 50 of the skull cavern without stairs. This will unlock the galaxy sword.

## Other changes

Most of those can be configured or turned off.

1. XP requirements are reduced for level 5 - 10.
2. Mr. Qi's room is unlocked immediately.
3. The first mastery is free.
4. The rock blocking the mountain can be destroyed early with a bomb or bronze pickaxe.
5. Villagers will gift you their personal weapon at 4 hearts.
6. Harvey gifts you the hot java ring at 6 hearts.
7. Monster musk is unlocked with level 7 combat.
8. Big monster eradication goals are reduced.
